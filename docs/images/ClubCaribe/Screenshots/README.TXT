Room 321 / The Squirrel's Nest - Club Caribe Picture Collection.

Most of these pictures were taken by Empathic and the rest by me.

All of the images are 320x128x16 colors with a file size under 10K. I have 
edited the pictures to show just the region area. Even the originals did not 
include the chat balloons. To get both requires doing either a video capture 
or taking a picture of your monitor. An example of the later can be seen at:
http://members.aol.com/qscreens/caribe/pit.jpg (by MarianneG)

When viewed at 640x480 the images are *about* half their orignal size. 
However, they are 20% wider because of the difference in the screen 
resolution ratio of the PC (1:1.333) and the C64 (1:1.6)
